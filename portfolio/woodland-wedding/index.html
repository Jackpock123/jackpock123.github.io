A unification of two great houses

Sir Paul and Dame Janet cordially invite you to their enchanting and mystical forest to witness and celebrate their unification of banners under the stars.

They would be honoured if you would join them for a day of wild festivities, with casks of the finest ale and wine from local markets, feasts, live music and of course the merriest of dancing into the night.
